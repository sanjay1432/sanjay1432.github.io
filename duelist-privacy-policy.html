Privacy Policy

DueList  ·  Last updated: March 2026

DueList is built on a simple principle: your financial data belongs to you, and only you. We have designed this app to work entirely offline with no data ever leaving your device.

1. Data Collection

DueList does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server or third party. All information you enter — subscription names, amounts, renewal dates, and ratings — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using the SQLite database engine provided by Expo SQLite. This data is sandboxed within the app and is not accessible to other apps. Uninstalling DueList will permanently delete all stored data.

3. Internet Access

DueList does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not make any network requests. There are no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or advertisement frameworks included in this application.

4. Third-Party Services

DueList does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data. The app has no advertising, no tracking pixels, and no social sign-in.

5. Children's Privacy

DueList does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this application is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and distributed via an app update on the Google Play Store. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the Contact Us option in the app's Settings screen.
